Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Overview

HouseMusicOnTheDaily ("we", "us", or "our") operates an automated social media posting service for the @housemusiconthedaily account. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our use of the TikTok API.

Information we collect

We do not collect personal information from end users or viewers. The only data we access is the TikTok account credentials (access token and open ID) necessary to authenticate with the TikTok Content Posting API on behalf of the account owner.

How we use information

The TikTok access token is used solely to publish pre-rendered video content to the @housemusiconthedaily TikTok account on an automated schedule. We do not use this information for any other purpose, and we do not share it with third parties.

Data storage

Access credentials are stored in a configuration file on a private server. They are not logged, transmitted to third parties, or used beyond the scope of automated video publishing.

Third-party services

This service uses the TikTok API. Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
